Weekend On-Call
Turn the in-patient report into CabMD claims. Every patient row on each day tab becomes a visit claim — A343A on the first day of the on-call block, A341A on later days — and each day's first patient adds C986A + C963A premiums while the rest add C987A. Day tabs are detected automatically, so a Friday, Monday or holiday Wednesday block works the same way.
