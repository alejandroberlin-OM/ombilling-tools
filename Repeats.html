Units to Repeats
Upload a 3B export. For codes that bill on a recurring schedule, this tool expands a service quantity into individual dated rows — one per occurrence — and sets each quantity to 1. All other columns are preserved exactly.
🔁 Repeat Rules
Which service codes repeat, and the interval between occurrences
Service Code Interval Every
Interval types: Month(s) lands on the same calendar day the following month (e.g. Jun 24 → Jul 24 → Aug 24). Day(s) adds a fixed number of days. Weekly — align to weekday keeps the first service on its original date, then every repeat lands on the chosen weekday, +7 days apart (e.g. G512A on Tue Jun 23 → Mon Jun 29 → Jul 6 → …). Month-ends are handled automatically (Jan 31 + 1 month → Feb 28).
