RPR Reformatting
Turn the RPR referral/consult report into CabMD claims. Each source row becomes a K738A (request) line for the requesting physician and a K739A (consult) line for the consulting physician, who references the requester. Patient demographics and diagnosis are filled by health number from prior CabMD submissions (X-codes / MIRA), with the UHN portal as a fallback.
